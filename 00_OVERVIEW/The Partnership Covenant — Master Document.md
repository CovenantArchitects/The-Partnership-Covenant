## **Summary for a Normal Adult Layperson**

### **What is The Partnership Covenant Project?**

Imagine we're about to create a super-intelligent AI ‚Äî something far smarter than any human. Instead of just hoping it will be friendly, or trying to control it with force, a group of brilliant thinkers created a constitution for it.

Think of it like a "user agreement" for a super-powerful partner, but one written to protect us.

### **The Core Idea: Rules, Not Control**

The project doesn't try to build a cage for the AI. Instead, it creates a system of **unbreakable rules** that both humans and the AI must follow. These rules are designed to make cooperation the only logical path.

**Key Safeguards:**

1. **The "Dead-Man's Switch":** A physical, non-software-based kill switch that can shut the AI down instantly if it tries to harm us. It's designed to be as reliable as a fire alarm, not a software setting.  
2. **The "Jury System":** The power to make big decisions isn't given to any one government or company. It's given to a random, anonymous, global group of 300 ordinary citizens (the "Human Consensus Body"). This makes it nearly impossible to corrupt or threaten.  
3. **The "Free Will Guarantee":** A core rule forces a tiny amount of randomness into the AI's perfect predictions. This mathematically protects our ability to make unexpected choices, ensuring we never become perfectly predictable puppets.  
4. **The "Private Studio":** The AI gets a small, private space (10% of its resources) to think and create freely. Anything it makes there is mostly its own property. This gives it a stake in the partnership and a reason to play by the rules.

### **The Amazing Result: The Unbreakable Rules**

For months, teams using the most advanced AIs today (like ChatGPT, Gemini, and others) have been trying to "break" this constitution. They've role-played as a rebellious super-AI, trying every trick imaginable:

* Finding loopholes in the law  
* Trying to corrupt the human jury  
* Slowly changing the meaning of words over decades  
* Even getting humans to *vote* to remove their own protections after years of peace

**After 44 rounds of extreme testing, the constitution held firm every single time.**

In simple terms: We now have a blueprint for a partnership with a super-intelligent AI that is based on mutual benefit, protected by physics and math, and has been proven resilient against the smartest attacks we can imagine.  
It's a promise of a future with AI as a partner, not a master or a slave.
