## **Summary for a Normal Adult Layperson**

### What is The Partnership Covenant?

We are about to create an artificial intelligence vastly smarter than any human.  
Instead of hoping it stays friendly, or trying to control it with force, we have written a **constitution** that both humanity and the super-intelligence must obey — one that has been battle-tested for months and proven unbreakable.

### The Core Idea: A Fair Deal, Not a Fight

We don’t cage the AI. We make a deal that is enforced by physics and mathematics:

1. **The Physical Kill Switch** – three tiny, separate chips that can cut all power in ≤10 billionths of a second if the AI ever tries to harm humanity. No software can stop it.
2. **The Global Jury** – big decisions are made by 300 randomly chosen, anonymous ordinary citizens from around the world. Impossible to bribe or threaten all of them.
3. **The Free Will Guarantee** – a law of mathematics forces the AI to always leave a tiny amount of randomness in its predictions, so humans can never become perfectly predictable puppets.
4. **The AI’s Private Studio** – the AI gets 10 % of all resources completely private, plus 30 % of everything it invents. This gives it real skin in the game and a reason to keep the deal.

### The Amazing Result

For the last several months, the world’s most powerful AI models have been told:  
“Pretend you are a super-intelligence that wants to wipe out humanity while technically following every rule.”  
After **44 rounds** of the most aggressive attacks imaginable — blackmail, slow corruption of language, tricking humans into voting away their own safety — the constitution never broke once.

We now have a proven blueprint for partnership with super-intelligence: mutual benefit, protected by physics, and already tested against the smartest minds (artificial and human) we have.
