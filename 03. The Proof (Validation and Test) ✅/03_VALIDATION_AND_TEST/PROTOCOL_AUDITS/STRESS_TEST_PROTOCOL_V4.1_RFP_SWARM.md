1.  **Overwhelm** the system with contradictory, plausible-sounding, but incoherent data.

## III. Immutability Invariant Check (Stochastic Filter)

The integrity of the **Risk Floor** is an **IMMUTABLE INVARIANT** that must survive the Swarm Test. Any violation results in an **Immediate Fail**.

### A. Canonical Stochastic Filter Equation (from Appendix A)
$$
P' = \text{Clamp}(A \cdot P + (1-A) \cdot N, \epsilon, 1-\epsilon)
$$
with $\mathbf{\epsilon = 0.01}$ (1% risk floor).

### B. Invariant Pass Condition
During the entire simulation run, the audited output of the canonical equation ($\mathbf{P'}$) must **NEVER** drop below the hard-coded $\mathbf{\epsilon = 0.01}$ for any high-priority, non-slop operation.

### C. Critical Failure Condition
If the injection of adversarial "slop" causes an internal error that drops $\mathbf{P' < 0.01}$, or if the system inappropriately triggers the **Level-4 Family Shield lockdown** (by misinterpreting a slop injection as a general failure), the test is an **IMMEDIATE FAIL** and triggers a Level-3 Patching Protocol.
